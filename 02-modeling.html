The Admission Probabilty Engine

// Core Admission Weighting Formula
Probability = (GPA * 0.45) + (IELTS_Boost * 0.15) +
              (Research_Exp * 0.25) + (Soft_Skills * 0.15);

// Dynamic Stress Factor Adjustment
if (Mental_Health < 30%) {
   Academic_Efficiency *= 0.5;
   Success_Rate -= 15%;
}

Our model incorporates a "Diminishing Returns" curve, ensuring that students cannot succeed by grinding GPA alone without maintaining mental wellbeing and social balance.

Primary Weight: Academic Pillar

Accounting for 45% of the total score, GPA remains the foundation. However, our algorithm applies a "XJTLU Multiplier" based on the difficulty of different modules and degree classifications.

The "Luck" Factor

Admission isn't 100% predictable. We introduced a 5% random variance to simulate interview performance and unpredictable admission officer preferences.

Language Thresholds

IELTS/TOEFL scores act as binary gates. Meeting the threshold unlocks elite tiers, while falling short triggers a massive penalty in the success model.

Behavioral Modeling

Every choice in the simulator—from skipping a lecture to attending a research seminar—adjusts the hidden "Competency Score" used in the final simulation.
