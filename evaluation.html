Final Reflection

A brief discussion on the social/ethical implications of our design and the use of AI in this project.

Social/Ethical Implications

  • Accessibility: The game is designed to be accessible to all students, regardless of their background or academic standing. We've ensured that the interface is intuitive and the content is clear and understandable.
  • Inclusivity: The game represents a diverse range of student experiences and backgrounds, promoting inclusivity and understanding of different perspectives.
  • Mental Health: We've incorporated elements that emphasize work-life balance and mental well-being, addressing the growing concern of student stress and burnout.
  • Educational Equity: The game provides valuable information about postgraduate preparation that may not be equally accessible to all students, helping to level the playing field.
  • Privacy: All user data is anonymous and used only for improving the game experience, with clear privacy guidelines.

Use of AI in This Project

We utilized generative AI in several aspects of this project:

  • Content Generation: AI was used to generate event descriptions, dialogue, and scenario variations, ensuring a diverse range of experiences for players.
  • Design Assistance: AI helped in generating UI design ideas and layout suggestions, which were then refined by our team.
  • Testing Support: AI-assisted in creating test scenarios and analyzing user feedback data.
  • Educational Content: AI helped in organizing and presenting complex information about postgraduate preparation in an engaging and accessible way.

All AI-generated content was reviewed and edited by our team to ensure accuracy, relevance, and alignment with our educational goals. We were careful to maintain human oversight throughout the development process to ensure the quality and appropriateness of all content.

Future Directions

Based on our evaluation and reflection, we plan to:

  • Expand the event library to include more diverse scenarios and challenges
  • Add multiplayer features to promote collaborative learning
  • Implement a more sophisticated AI system to provide personalized guidance
  • Develop additional modules for specific fields of study
  • Create a mobile version to increase accessibility
