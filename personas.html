Personas

Zhenyu Chen

20-year-old XJTLU undergraduate

Background

Zhenyu Chen is a second-year undergraduate majoring in Electrical Engineering at XJTLU. He is starting to think about postgraduate studies but has limited knowledge about the application process.

Goals

  • Understand the postgraduate application process early
  • Develop good study habits and time management skills
  • Explore different postgraduate program options
  • Prepare effectively for future application requirements

Pain Points

  • Lack of knowledge about application requirements
  • Uncertainty about career paths
  • Difficulty in planning long-term academic goals
  • Limited exposure to postgraduate program options

How the Simulator Helps

The XJTLU Journey Simulator helps Zhenyu Chen by providing early exposure to the application process, teaching effective time management strategies, and offering insights into different postgraduate paths, allowing him to make more informed decisions about his academic future.

Ziheng Li

21-year-old XJTLU undergraduate

Background

Ziheng Li is a third-year undergraduate majoring in Information and Computing Science at XJTLU. He is interested in pursuing a master's degree but is unsure about the application process and requirements.

Goals

  • Understand the postgraduate application process
  • Learn how to balance academics and extracurricular activities
  • Improve his English proficiency for IELTS
  • Gain insights into different postgraduate programs

Pain Points

  • Uncertainty about application requirements
  • Difficulty in time management
  • Stress about English proficiency
  • Lack of guidance on program selection

How the Simulator Helps

The XJTLU Journey Simulator helps Ziheng Li by providing a realistic simulation of the application process, allowing him to practice time management, and offering guidance on balancing different aspects of his academic life.
