Usability Testing

We conducted task-based tests with 10 students across different majors. Users were asked to reach a Top 10 University offer within the simulator. 9 out of 10 succeeded on their first attempt, proving the UI's intuitiveness.

Key Findings

The "AI Advisor" was rated as the most helpful feature, while users suggested that the "Map Navigation" could be even more interactive in future updates.

Iterative Changes

Based on testing feedback, we simplified the GPA calculation display and added "Quick Tips" for first-time users. We also improved the responsiveness of the Energy rings to provide better immediate feedback upon task completion.

Iterative Reflection

Developing PathFinder was more than a technical challenge; it was a lesson in Human-Centric Computing. We learned that data alone isn't enough—it must be presented in a way that reduces anxiety rather than increasing it.


Our greatest takeaway is that empathy in design matters. By gamifying the stressful application process, we've enabled students to explore their potential in a safe, simulated environment before making life-changing decisions.
