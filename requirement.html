User Journey Map

Actor & Scenario

Actor: XJTLU undergraduate students preparing for postgraduate studies

Scenario: Students aim to navigate their academic journey, build a strong profile, and secure offers from their dream universities.

Phases

Phase 1: Onboarding

Setting goals and creating character

Phase 2: Exploration

Exploring campus and activities

Phase 3: Development

Building skills and profile

Phase 4: Outcome

Receiving offers and endings

Actions & Thoughts

Phase 1: Onboarding

Actions: Create character, set postgraduate goals, choose desired universities

Thoughts: "What do I want to study? Which universities are best for me?"

Phase 2: Exploration

Actions: Explore campus buildings, participate in activities, meet characters

Thoughts: "What activities will help me build my profile? How can I balance my time?"

Phase 3: Development

Actions: Manage time, make decisions, develop skills, build network

Thoughts: "Am I making the right decisions? Am I on track for my goals?"

Phase 4: Outcome

Actions: Receive offers, reflect on journey, plan next steps

Thoughts: "Did I achieve my goals? What could I have done differently?"

Emotional Journey

Students experience a range of emotions throughout their journey, from initial excitement and curiosity to periods of stress and uncertainty, finally reaching a sense of achievement and reflection at the end.

Opportunities

Academic Planning

Provide personalized academic advising and course selection guidance based on career goals.

Skill Development

Offer skill-building workshops and resources tailored to specific fields of study.

Networking

Facilitate connections with alumni, professors, and industry professionals.

Well-being Support

Provide resources for stress management and work-life balance during the application process.

Requirements List

Must-Haves for a Playful System

1

Engaging Game Mechanics

Interactive elements that make the learning process fun and engaging, including exploration, decision-making, and feedback systems.

2

Personalized Experience

Customizable character creation and tailored content based on individual goals and interests.

3

Meaningful Feedback

Clear and constructive feedback on decisions and progress, helping students understand the impact of their choices.
