"The white interface feels like a boring school portal."

Navigation Refinement

Users struggled with the map. We added a "Quick-Travel" sidebar to streamline movement across campus buildings.

AI Assistant Integration

Initial chat was too static. We moved it to a dedicated AI sidebar with robot avatars for better personality.
